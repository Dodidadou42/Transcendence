What is Pong?

Pong is one of the earliest video games ever created. It's a simple game that simulates table tennis in a digital format. Each player controls a digital paddle and the goal is to hit the ball in such a way that your opponent cannot return it.

Game Rules

Here are the basic rules for playing Pong:

  • Game Objective: The goal is to beat your opponent by being the first to reach 10 points. A player scores a point each time the opponent misses the ball.
  • Paddle Control: The player controls a paddle by moving it vertically on the left or right side of the screen. They can compete against another player controlling a second paddle on the opposite side.
  • Gameplay: Players use the paddles to hit the ball back and forth.
